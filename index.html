What We Do

OUR Carpentry
SERVICES

All carpentry work done at your home — custom builds, installations, and repairs. We bring tools, skill, and experience. No materials shop, just craftsmen.

01 / 09
🪑
Custom Furniture Making
Built to your measurements, made to last

We design and build wooden furniture to fit your exact space and lifestyle. Precise measurements, your design and budget, built on-site. Beds, dining tables, study desks, TV units, coffee tables, wooden shelving — every piece made to order with clean joints and smooth finishes throughout.

  • Custom Dining Tables & Chairs
  • Wooden Bed Frames
  • Study Desks & Work Tables
  • TV & Entertainment Units
  • Coffee & Side Tables
  • Bespoke Wooden Shelves
02 / 09
🚪
Door Repair & Installation
Every door perfectly hung and secured

Sticking, rattling, broken or misaligned doors are a frustration and a security problem. We install new wooden, flush, and panel doors from scratch and repair existing ones — fixing warped frames, broken hinges, faulty locks, door closers, handles, and peepholes. Every door left smooth and secure.

  • New Wooden Door Installation
  • Door Frame Repair
  • Hinge Replacement & Alignment
  • Lock Installation & Repair
  • Peephole & Closer Fitting
  • Sticking Door Repair
03 / 09
🪟
Window Repair & Installation
Smooth, weathertight windows every time

Rattling frames, jammed tracks, broken latches, and cracked wooden surrounds worsen with every Delhi season. We repair and install wooden, aluminium, and sliding windows — smooth operation, correct sealing, proper alignment throughout. Glass panels, handles, hinges, sliding mechanisms — all repaired or replaced.

  • Wooden Window Installation
  • Sliding Track Repair
  • Hinge & Latch Fixing
  • Glass Panel Replacement
  • Frame Repair & Sealing
  • Alignment Correction
04 / 09
🗄️
Wardrobe & Almirah Work
Perfect storage, precisely fitted

We build new wardrobes to your room's exact dimensions and repair existing ones fully. Built-in fitted units, sliding door wardrobes, open-shelf systems, traditional almirahs — all built with precise fitting. We also fix jammed drawers, bent hinges, collapsed shelves, and off-track sliding doors quickly and durably.

  • Custom Built-In Wardrobes
  • Wardrobe Assembly & Installation
  • Sliding Door Repair & Alignment
  • Drawer Channel Replacement
  • Hinge, Lock & Handle Work
  • Shelf Repair & Reinforcement
05 / 09
🍳
Modular Kitchen Installation
Functional kitchens fitted to perfection

Modular kitchens demand precise carpentry — level cabinets, clean-sliding drawers, soft-close hinges, and comfortable handle positioning. We assemble and install all modular kitchen units, fit pull-out and push-open mechanisms, repair moisture-damaged cabinets, and install all associated hardware with accuracy and care.

  • Full Modular Kitchen Installation
  • Cabinet Assembly & Fitting
  • Drawer & Pull-Out Mechanisms
  • Soft-Close Hinge Fitting
  • Handle & Knob Installation
  • Existing Kitchen Repair
06 / 09
🛏️
Bed Assembly & Repair
Solid, stable beds — built correctly

A wobbly or collapsing bed is uncomfortable and unsafe. We assemble all types of beds correctly — Indian wooden frames, Chinese knock-down beds, box beds with storage, bunk beds, and cot frames. We also repair existing beds by tightening joints, fixing headboards, replacing broken slats, and restoring full stability.

  • Wooden Bed Frame Assembly
  • Box Bed & Storage Bed Work
  • Bunk Bed Assembly
  • Headboard & Footboard Fixing
  • Squeaky Joint Repair
  • Bed Support & Slat Work
07 / 09
🔧
Furniture Repair & Restoration
Fix properly before you replace

Before spending on new furniture, let us assess what you have. We fix broken chair joints, wobbly tables, damaged cabinet panels, sagging drawer channels, broken sofa frames, and worn polish surfaces. Quality repair extends good furniture's life by years at a fraction of replacement cost — and the result looks and feels new.

  • Chair & Table Joint Repair
  • Cabinet Restoration
  • Sofa Frame Repair
  • Drawer Channel Replacement
  • Panel Repair & Patching
  • Wood Polish & Finishing
08 / 09
📚
Bookshelf & Storage Units
Fitted storage for every room

Custom storage transforms a home. We build and install wall-mounted bookshelves, floor-to-ceiling storage walls, floating shelves, under-stair storage, and multi-purpose display cabinets — all fitted precisely to your wall dimensions. We also assemble and stabilise freestanding shelving and repair existing storage that is unsafe.

  • Wall-Mounted Bookshelf Fitting
  • Floor-to-Ceiling Storage
  • Floating Shelf Installation
  • Under-Stair Storage
  • Freestanding Shelf Assembly
  • Storage Cabinet Construction

General Carpentry & Repairs
Every wood job at home, done well

We handle all everyday carpentry tasks that keep a home running properly. Curtain rod and blind installation, wall and ceiling hanger fitting, lock and latch work, safety gate installation, minor wooden repairs throughout the home, polish and finishing work, and any custom on-request carpentry that needs an experienced hand to do right.

  • Curtain Rod & Blind Installation
  • Wall & Ceiling Hanger Fitting
  • Lock, Latch & Bolt Work
  • Safety Gate Installation
  • Polish & Wood Finishing
  • Custom Repairs On-Request
