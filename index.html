What We Do

OUR Services

Complete carpentry under one roof — from custom furniture and kitchens to repairs, installations, and woodwork of every kind. All work done at your home, on your schedule.

Custom Furniture Making
Designed for your space, built to last

We design and build bespoke wooden furniture tailored exactly to your space and specifications. Dining tables, beds, study desks, TV units, coffee tables — every piece is measured, crafted, and finished to perfection using quality wood and traditional joinery techniques.

  • Custom Dining Tables
  • Wooden Bed Frames
  • Study & Work Desks
  • TV Units & Entertainment Centres
  • Coffee Tables & Side Tables
  • Wooden Shelves & Bookcases

Door Repair & Installation
Smooth, secure, perfectly aligned

We handle all types of door work — repairing stiff, sagging, or damaged doors and installing new wooden, flush, or panel doors from scratch. Broken hinges, faulty locks, misaligned frames — all fixed with precision. We also install peepholes, door closers, handles, and security fittings.

  • Wooden Door Installation
  • Door Hinge Replacement
  • Door Lock Repair & Installation
  • Door Frame Fixing
  • Peephole Installation
  • Door Closer Installation

🪟
Window Repair & Installation
Tight, aligned, weatherproof

Rattling windows, broken latches, jammed tracks, and cracked frames — our team handles all of it. We repair and install wooden, aluminium, and sliding windows, ensuring they open smoothly and seal properly. We also fix hinges, handles, glass panels, and sliding track mechanisms.

  • Wooden Window Installation
  • Aluminium & Sliding Window Repair
  • Window Hinge & Lock Fixing
  • Glass Panel Replacement
  • Window Frame Repairs
  • Track & Alignment Fixing

Wardrobe & Almirah Work
More storage, better organisation

We assemble, install, and repair all types of wardrobes — from simple Indian almirahs to modern sliding door wardrobes and built-in fitted units. Whether you need a new wardrobe installed or a broken drawer fixed, our team delivers results that look great and function perfectly for years.

  • Wardrobe Assembly & Installation
  • Sliding Door Wardrobe Fixing
  • Built-In Custom Wardrobes
  • Hinge & Lock Repair
  • Drawer Channel Replacement
  • Handle & Knob Installation

  • Kitchen Cabinet Assembly
  • Drawer & Shelf Installation
  • Soft-Close Hinge Fitting
  • Pull-Out Mechanism Fixing
  • Kitchen Handle Installation
  • Existing Kitchen Repairs

  • Box Bed Installation
